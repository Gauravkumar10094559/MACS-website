About Asymptotes

ASYMPTOTES - Defining New Boundaries... is the annual Technical-fest organised by Maths And Computing Society - MACS-DTU.
ASYMPTOTES marks the advent of metamorphosis of bookworms to smart thinking professionals.

ASYMPTOTES is indeed going to DEFINE NEW BOUNDARIES with its plenitude of technical events like programming contests, web designing, quizzes and ever widening array of technical and informal events such as Sudoku, Ken-Ken, Rubik's cube, Giant chess and Laser Guiding.

Bringing a respite from the hard curriculum of engineering, the students shall give their best in making this fest a success story. Mathematics acts as a connecting link among all branches of engineering and therefore this endurance of the members of MACS in organizing.

ASYMPTOTES is going to reap wonders for them which is why ASYMPTOTES is here to stay.
