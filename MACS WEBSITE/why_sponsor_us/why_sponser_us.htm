Why sponsor us?

The Mathematics and Computing Society blooms in its fullest vigour at the time of its annual techno-cultural festival, Asymptotes. Attended by a flood of students from engineering colleges across the country, the mega event registers attendees in an enormous amount. Such a lively environment brings with it, amazing opportunities for promotion and advertisement.
Separate stalls for our sponsors are arranged around the lawns and lobbies. They act as magnets for students who wish to know more about the companies and the services which may benefit them. And indeed, the students never get enough of it. The 'food joints' of our catering partners have been all-time crowd pullers for the trencherman in each. Besides these, we invite our sponsors to show their promotional presentations in the middle of popular events which guarantee them a packed auditorium. The space we provide to our sponsors in the posters and hoardings is visible in the various colleges in Delhi and the NCR region. And finally, our media partners leave no stone unturned while publicizing our sponsors in general public or corporate lobbies via radio, internet or print media.

  • Your Brand becomes synonymous with the memories that participants carry back home from the Asymptotes experience.
  • Business growth and expansion opportunities.
  • Extensive Brand Exposure.
  • Opportunity to witness some of the brightest minds to showcase their talent.
  • Enhance corporate interaction.
  • Direct product (and service) usage and showcasing.

The glamour, business opportunities and exposure aside, we believe in indulging with our heart and soul with our guests. Constant feedback from our sponsors has made sure that we work towards a win-win situation; wherein both parties are mutually benefited. A team of volunteers is forever at the ready to make certain that all goes with the expectations of the sponsor. Proper care is taken to ensure that the promotional teams are provided with lunch and snacks at regular intervals

A legacy of success precedes us and a promising future awaits us. As with Asymptotes; we are working to make it bigger and better, every year.
